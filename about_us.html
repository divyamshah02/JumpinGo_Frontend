How it started

JUMPIN GO began with a simple idea: give kids a safe place to go crazy with energy without screens and gadgets. The founders combined their experience in events and family entertainment to design a park that feels like a mega-fun festival, but is easy to access all year round.

Where we are today

Today, JUMPINGO stands as the biggest inflatable carnival in Ahmedabad — and one of the largest bouncing house experiences in India. Designed for all age groups from 5 to 55, it brings an unforgettable blend of fun, thrill, and adventure under one massive arena.
