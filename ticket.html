Sky Swing

Experience the ultimate thrill at JumpinGo! Soar high on our Sky Swing, enjoy gravity-defying fun on trampolines, and test your limits on exciting obstacle courses. Perfect for families, friends, and adrenaline seekers. With state-of-the-art safety, vibrant atmosphere, and endless entertainment, JumpinGo promises unforgettable memories. Book your ticket now and leap into adventure!

2500/- per person
  • Height Requirement: Minimum 4 feet
  • Age Limit: 8 years and above
  • Duration: 5 minutes per ride
  • Capacity: 24 riders simultaneously
  • Safety: Full body harness and secure locking system

The Sky Swing is our signature attraction that lifts you 100 feet in the air while spinning at exhilarating speeds. Feel the wind rush past as you swing out over the park, creating memories that will last a lifetime. This ride combines the classic swing carousel with modern thrills, perfect for both adventure seekers and those looking for scenic views of JumpinGo park.
