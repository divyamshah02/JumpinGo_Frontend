Ticket One

Experience pure joy as families and friends bounce together in JUMPINGO's specially designed zones. Perfect for ages 5 to 55, this family-friendly session combines age-appropriate inflatables with supervised safety zones where everyone – kids, parents, and grandparents – can jump, play, and connect away from screens.

1999 INR + GST Per person

  • Duration: 5PM to 10PM
  • Age Range: 5-55 years
  • Maximum Capacity: 1500 people per slot
  • Supervision: Trained safety staff on-ground at all times
  • Entry Requirements: Clean socks mandatory (no bare feet or shoes)

What's Included: Access to curated inflatable zones with color-coordinated difficulty levels. Separate soft-landing areas for younger jumpers, advanced obstacle sections for teens and adults, common zones for group activities, and cool-down spaces. Staff presence throughout. Photo-friendly environment.

  • Food & Beverage: Snacks and drinks available at venue
  • Corporate Events: Team-building packages starting from 40 people
